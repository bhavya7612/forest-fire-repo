It's crucial to take immediate action to prevent and manage wildfires. Here are the steps you should consider:
  1. Implement Fire Restrictions:
    • Enforce fire bans or restrictions in the affected areas.
    • Prohibit open burning, campfires, and other activities that could ignite fires.

  2. Increase Patrols and Surveillance:
    • Deploy additional personnel to monitor the forest.
    • Conduct regular patrols to detect any signs of smoke or fire.

  3. Preposition Resources:
    • Position firefighting equipment strategically.
    • Ensure that water tanks, hoses, and other tools are readily available.

  4. Assess Vulnerable Areas:
    • Identify critical zones such as residential neighborhoods, wildlife habitats, and infrastructure.
    • Prioritize protection efforts based on the FWI and potential fire behavior.

  5. Prepare for Evacuation:
    • Develop evacuation plans for at-risk communities.
    • Communicate evacuation routes and assembly points.
